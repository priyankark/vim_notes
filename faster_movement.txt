To move from word to word: w
To go back a word: b
To move from words separated by spaces: W and B
To go to end of line: $
To go to beginning of line: ^ First non space letter. 0 to real beginning
To beginning of file: gg
To end of file: G
From paragraph to paragraph: }
To find letter P say: fP
To go back to find: F and letter
Character before something: t<ch>
Repeater: numberfw : find the second w instead of first
Use this for all commands.
Numbers alongside text-> :set number
To go to certain line-> :<number>
